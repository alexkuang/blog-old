About

Me

Hi, I’m a software rockstar ninja code wizard who rides unicorns (though that should all go without saying). A recent college grad turned journeyman developer, my goal is to reduce the amount of painful tedium in the world with software that doesn’t suck. And (this is very very important) I am a madman with a keyboard.

Tech-wise, my current love is Scala, but I’ve also tinkered with everything from Python to Ruby to Perl, even though I try my hardest to forget about that last one. My preferred work environment is a Macbook Pro, and my favorite text editor is vim. Simple enough, but for more information on my technical background, my full resume can be found here.

When not staring at a screen, I’m usually experimenting with food, photographing the aforementoned food, and training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.

This Site

This is my personal blog and will contain thoughts about everything from code to the state of the software industry to life in general. It will also be an attempt to restart food blogging after it got completely pole-axed by the stampede known as Meaningful Use Certification. I will mix metaphors, occasionally use the second person, and probably hurl a lot of invectives depending on the topic. But, above all, I will be honest.

Everything is driven by Octopress and hosted on GitHub Pages. The theme is whitespace.
